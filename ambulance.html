Emergency Information

Signs: Chest pain/pressure, pain in arms/back/neck, shortness of breath, cold sweat, nausea

First Aid: Call for ambulance immediately, have person sit or lie down, loosen tight clothing

Steps:

  1. Ensure your safety first
  2. Call for emergency services
  3. Don't move injured persons unless necessary
  4. Apply pressure to bleeding wounds
